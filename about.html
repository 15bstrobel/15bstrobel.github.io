About Brian Strobel
I'm a sophomore in UW CSE, currently taking CSE 311 Foundations of Computing, MATH 308 Matrix Algebra, and CHEM 152 General Chemistry. I'm also a TA for the intro CS courses. This past summer I worked at iD Tech teaching kids about Java programming, Minecraft modding, and other awesome tech stuff. I enjoy gamedev (as this website should make apparent), but I've recently become more interested in the more practical application of computer science.

My goal is to become a Software Developer. Ideally, I also want to gain research experience in Machine Learning/Big Data, as this will likely be a big deal in the near future. I enjoyed reading Pedro Domingos's book on the subject and I hope to learn more in my junior and senior years of college. Realistically, however, there are many fascinating computer science topics that I'm eager to learn over the next few years, machine learning is just one of them.
