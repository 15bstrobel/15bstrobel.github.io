About Brian Strobel
I'm a freshmen at UW and my intended major is computer science. My favorite hobby is game development; growing up I used to make a lot of board games, but now I mostly work on computer games. This website is an outlet for my creativity and a way for me to document my progress towards the computer science major.

My major goals are to get accepted into UW's computer science program, find an internship or tech-related job, and develop the skills necessary to start making multiplayer/networked games. My next project is going to utilize Firebase and this website to make a turn-based game. If you are at UW and want to join my project, you can email me at bstrobel@uw.edu. I'm also looking for a job or internship, so recruiters can use that email as well.
