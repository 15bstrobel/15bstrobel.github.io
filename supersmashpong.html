Super Smash Pong
Super Smash Pong is a work in progress; I'm making it with Akash and Sarthak is a terminated project. I think we had some good ideas, but unfortunately the team did not have good chemistry. Proper execution is critical in gamedev, probably more important than the idea itself. Think about it‐there are millions of ideas for games that exist, but only a few become successful. How many random iOS apps are sitting with 20 downloads and no ratings? Brilliant ideas are NOTHING compared to the actual execution. A game needs JUICE, passion, balanced mechanics, and an absolutely perfect user experience. Anything less than that and an idea will fail.
